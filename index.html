Indra AI: AI-Powered Teaching Assistant for Content Creation and Multilingual Localization

Shivam Sharma  ·  India AI Summit 2026  ·  Case Study 17

This case study presents Indra AI's generative-AI teaching assistant, designed to reduce teacher workload while expanding access to high-quality multilingual learning materials across India's K–12 classrooms. Integrated with open-source platforms, the system produces curriculum-aligned lesson plans, worksheets, quizzes, and stories in multiple Indian languages enabling activity-based learning at scale while preserving public-sector governance and data sovereignty.
